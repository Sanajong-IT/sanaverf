Our Services

A complete solution for your renovation and painting needs.

Interior Painting

Our interior painting services refresh your living spaces with beautiful, even coats of paint. We take the time to prepare surfaces properly, repair imperfections, and apply high‑quality eco‑friendly paints for a lasting finish.

  • Walls, ceilings, trim, and cabinetry
  • Low‑VOC and eco‑friendly paint options
  • Protection of furniture and flooring

Exterior Painting

Boost your curb appeal with our durable exterior painting services. We provide thorough surface preparation, including power washing and scraping, followed by the application of weather‑resistant paints and finishes.

  • Residential and commercial exteriors
  • Wood, brick, stucco, and metal surfaces
  • Weather‑proof coatings for long‑term protection

Renovation Projects

From minor updates to full remodels, our renovation services transform outdated rooms into stunning, functional spaces. We manage every phase, from design to construction, ensuring the process is seamless and the results are spectacular.

  • Kitchen and bathroom remodeling
  • Basement finishing and room additions
  • Custom carpentry and built‑ins

Flooring & Tiling

Upgrade your floors with expert installation of hardwood, laminate, vinyl, or tile. We help you choose the right materials for your lifestyle and budget, ensuring a flawless and beautiful finish.

  • Hardwood and engineered wood flooring
  • Laminate, vinyl planks, and tile
  • Professional removal and disposal of old flooring

Surface Prep & Drywall Repair

A flawless finish starts with proper preparation. We provide comprehensive surface preparation services, including sanding, plastering, and drywall repair, to ensure your walls are smooth and ready for paint or wallpaper.

  • Patch holes, cracks, and water damage
  • Skim coating and texturing
  • Priming and sealing

Color Consultation & Design

Choosing the right colors and finishes can be overwhelming. Our designers provide expert guidance, helping you create a cohesive color palette that reflects your personal style and enhances your home’s value.

  • Personalized color schemes
  • Material and finish recommendations
  • Decor and styling advice
